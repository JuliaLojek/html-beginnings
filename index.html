The Coldest Summer

Have you ever considered visiting Iceland? Maybe you've been there and would like to know if my experience was different?

I spent five months on this island. Living, working, travelling, meeting Icelanders and people from all around the world.

I also took a huge amount of photos (yes, it is SO beautiful there) and discovered places that weren't crowded at all (which is more and more difficult these days).

My name is Julia and on this website you can find some useful information about living in Iceland and travelling around this awesome piece of land. I share my experience, photos and recommendations.

I hope you'll find here something for yourself. Enjoy! :)
